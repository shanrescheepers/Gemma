"You can hear music, and feel it, but to visualise it without insturments... is another dimension of creation."

The concept behind Gemma arose as a solution for individuals unable to play traditional instruments due to limitations in accessibility, capabilities and creative freedom,
enabling anyone to collaborate and create music alongside those proficient in live instrument playing without limitations.

While the current version of the app has been developed and is functional, we anticipate ongoing enhancements and improvements to further enhance the user experience and expand its features in the future, allowing for two handed gestures simultaneously!
